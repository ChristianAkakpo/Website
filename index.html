About Our School

ABREWANKOR D/A BASIC SCHOOL was established in 1975 and is located opposite the Roman Catholic Church at Abrewankor in the Krachi Nchumuru District of the Oti Region.

The school began under sheds and trees, gradually moving to a classroom block through the help of the Ghana government and some stakeholders of the school.

With dedicated teachers, modern teaching methods, and a conducive learning environment, we strive to bring out the best in every child entrusted to our care. Our academic performance in the Basic Education Certificate Examination has consistently been the best in the circuit.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and inclusive learning environment that empowers students to achieve their full potential,develop a love for learning , and become responsible citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that fosters academic excellence,creativity, and character development,preparing students for a brighter future and a lifetime of success.

Junior High School

Our JHS program prepares students for secondary education with rigorous academic training and character development.

Extracurricular Activities

BEYOND THE CLASSROOM

At Abrewankor D/A Basic School, we are dedicated to delivering a holistic form of education that integrates academic excellence with extracurricular engagement. We encourage our students to participate in sports, cultural programs, and club activities, which play a vital role in fostering their physical development, social interaction, and emotional well-being.

These experiences help them stay active, build confidence, and develop socially and emotionally, while preparing them for lifelong success.

Our Staff

DEDICATED TEACHING PROFESSIONALS

Our school has young, active, experienced and energetic teachers, currently totaling 10 staff members. Out of these, 3 are female and 7 are male. All teachers in the school are professional.

The school's leadership is flexible and takes into account processes, decisions, and actions that ensure the school's goals are achieved. These are strictly monitored and supervised by the headteacher with other stakeholders like PTA and SMC, through the help of the District Education Office.

Admissions

Join Our School Community

We welcome applications from parents and guardians seeking quality education for their wards. Admission is open to children from kindergarten through junior high school.

Requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report (for transfers)
  • One passport-sized photograph

For more information about our admission process, and requirements, please contact the school administration.
